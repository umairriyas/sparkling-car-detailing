Sparkling Car Detailing

Pricing, Services & Mobile Policy

Our Pricing Philosophy

At Sparkling Car Detailing, our pricing serves as a guideline and is based on vehicles presented in well-maintained, average condition.

Every vehicle we detail is unique. Vehicles requiring additional time, specialised techniques, or enhanced processes — including those with heavy soiling, excessive contamination, or pet hair — will incur additional charges to reflect the extra work involved.

Pet hair removal is not included in standard packages and is assessed separately due to the time, skill, and specialised tools required to achieve our standard of finish.

Where applicable, any pricing adjustments will be clearly communicated and approved prior to commencement, ensuring full transparency and no unexpected costs.

Final pricing is determined based on an on-site visual assessment, or a review of photos or video provided. This approach allows us to consistently deliver the premium results Sparkling Car Detailing is known for, without compromise.

Optional Add-On Services

The following services are not included in standard detailing packages and are available as optional add-ons:

  • Engine Bay Detailing
  • Black Trim Restoration
  • Headlight Restoration
  • Cut & Polish

These services require additional time, specialised products, and professional techniques and are priced separately. Availability and pricing will be confirmed at the time of booking or prior to commencement of work.

Workshop & Mobile Detailing Options

Sparkling Car Detailing offers clients two service options, allowing flexibility without compromising on quality.

Workshop Detailing

Vehicles are brought to our fully equipped detailing studio for service.

Workshop Location: 3 / 29 Chester Road, Altona, VIC 3018

Workshop detailing does not incur any call-out or travel fees and is recommended for clients seeking the most controlled environment and optimal results.

Mobile Detailing

We also offer a professional mobile detailing service, attending your home or workplace by appointment.

Mobile detailing is available subject to the following distance-based call-out fees, calculated from our Altona workshop location:

  • Up to 30 km: $50 call-out fee
  • 30 km to 60 km: $80 call-out fee

Mobile services outside this range may be available by prior arrangement only.

Any applicable call-out fee will be clearly advised at the time of booking and confirmed prior to commencement of work.

Clients may choose workshop service at any time to avoid mobile call-out fees.
