Who We Are

Sparkling Car Detailing provides professional car detailing services for a wide range of clients, delivering consistent, high-quality results through both our workshop detailing & mobile detailing options.

Workshop & Mobile Detailing Options

Workshop Detailing

Our workshop offers a dedicated, fully equipped environment for clients who prefer to bring their vehicle to us.

Mobile Detailing

Our mobile service delivers the same professional standard of care at your home or workplace for added convenience.

Available across Melbourne

Clients may choose between workshop detailing or mobile detailing based on their preferences, schedule, and service requirements. Ongoing maintenance plans and scheduled fleet servicing are available upon request.

Who We Service

Professional detailing for diverse client needs across Melbourne

Private Vehicle Owners

Including everyday vehicles, family cars, pre-sale vehicles, prestige vehicles & enthusiast-owned cars.

Clients may choose either workshop detailing or mobile detailing to suit their needs and availability.

Company & Fleet Vehicles

We service company vehicles, business fleets & work vehicles that require a clean, professional presentation.

Our services are suitable for both one-off detailing & ongoing vehicle care.

Car Dealerships & Automotive Businesses

We work with car dealerships and automotive businesses requiring professional vehicle presentation, including pre-sale detailing, handover preparation & presentation work.

Services are available via both workshop & mobile options, depending on requirements.

Professionals & Client Facing Drivers

Including ride-share drivers, chauffeurs & professionals whose vehicles represent their business where attention to detail matters.

Both workshop and mobile detailing options are available to suit individual schedules and preferences.
